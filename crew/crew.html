ANOUSHEH ANSARI

Meet your crew Flight Engineer Anousheh Ansari Anousheh Ansari is an Iranian
American engineer and co-founder of Prodea Systems. Ansari was the fourth self-
funded space tourist, the first self-funded woman to fly
to the ISS, and the first Iranian in space.
